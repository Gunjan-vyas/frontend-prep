What is Moneyview UPI?

Moneyview has partnered with NPCI to provide UPI services to our valuable customers. Moneyview is one of the fastest UPI apps, with guaranteed security for your payments. You can now add a bank account or a RuPay credit card to make seamless payments.

Is Moneyview UPI safe?

Moneyview UPI is fully compliant with RBI & NPCI regulations. We ensure the safety of your data with 256 bit data encryption. Moneyview is also certified with Information Security Management System ISO/IEC 27001:2022. We also use advanced technology to identify fraud and spam to enable safe and secure transactions.

Are there any charges on using UPI on Moneyview?

Moneyview UPI is absolutely free of cost, without any hidden charges.

How can I register on Moneyview UPI?

You can register on Moneyview UPI by following these steps:

  • Tap on "Activate UPI" banner on Moneyview Home Page
  • Grant permission to send SMS (if not already given)
  • Select the SIM card with your registered mobile number on Moneyview
  • Select the bank on which you have opened a Savings account


You will see your Moneyview UPI ID along with the bank details in the final screen.

I am facing issues during registration of UPI on Moneyview

Please make sure that you have granted all the necessary permissions to register on Moneyview UPI. You also have to make sure that you are selecting the correct SIM with the mobile number which is registered on Moneyview.
If the issue still persists, please retry again after 24 hours as the maximum attempts of registering on UPI as per NPCI is 3 attempts within 24 hours.

How can I add a bank account on Moneyview UPI?

You can add your bank accounts during UPI registration. Moneyview will fetch the bank accounts that are registered with your mobile number.
You can also add another bank account by going to the UPI Dashboard:
- Tap on "Settings" icon on UPI Dashboard
- Tap on "Bank Account" icon on the UPI Settings screen
- Tap on "Add Bank Account" and select the bank name you wish to add
- Confirm the details and tap on "Done"
Your bank account will be added to Moneyview UPI. You can make this your primary account if you wish to receive funds on this account using your Moneyview UPI ID.

How can I add RuPay Credit Card on Moneyview UPI?

You can add your RuPay credit card after linking your bank account while registering.
Follow the below steps on the UPI dashboard:
- Tap on the "Settings" icon
- Tap on "Add RuPay Credit Card"
- Select the Bank in which you have an active RuPay Credit Card
- Confirm the card details on the final screen
Your RuPay credit card will be linked to Moneyview. You can now use this card to make UPI payments on any merchant.

Can I add other credit cards on UPI?

Currently, NPCI has enabled only RuPay Credit Card for UPI services.

My transaction failed, what do I do now?

Once the transaction is failed, you can check your account balance to see if the amount has been debited. In most cases, the amount does not get debited in failed transactions.
If the amount is debited, you can tap on the "Raise complaint" icon and raise a transaction dispute to NPCI using your Moneyview app. Once you raise a dispute, you will get an estimated time of closure of the dispute on the app and you can check the status of the dispute on the app.

How do I report a fraudulent transaction?

You can report a fraudulent transaction by following the steps given below:
- Tap on the "View Transaction History" icon on UPI Dashboard
- Select the transaction you want to report
- Tap on the "Raise complaint" icon (or Raise Dispute icon)
- Enter the details asked on the screen
You will get a confirmation on the screen with your Service Request number and estimated time of closure directly from NPCI.
You can track the status of the transaction regularly from the same screen.

Where can I see my QR code?

You can see your QR code by following the below steps:
- Go to UPI Dashboard
- Tap on the "QR code" icon beside your UPI ID
You will be able to see your QR code, download it, and share it too.

How do I set UPI Autopay on Moneyview UPI?

You can setup UPI Autopay on Moneyview by entering your Moneyview UPI ID on the merchant checkout page. You can also select the "Moneyview" icon inside the same checkout page (if available). The other option is to scan the QR code which contains the Autopay details.
